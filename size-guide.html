How to Measure

01
Chest
Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
02
Waist
Measure around your natural waistline, just above the hip bone.
03
Hips
Measure around the widest part of your hips and seat.
04
Inseam
Measure from the crotch seam down to the ankle along the inside leg.
