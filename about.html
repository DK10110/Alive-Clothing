How It Started

More Than
Just Clothes

ALIVE Clothing was founded in Sri Lanka in 2023 with one simple vision — to create fashion that feels modern, confident, and real.

What started with imported clothing for men and women quickly became something bigger: a growing lifestyle brand built around individuality, comfort, and everyday style.

At ALIVE, we believe clothing should do more than just look good. It should make you feel confident every time you wear it.
